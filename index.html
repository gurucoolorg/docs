Overview

GuruCool is a generic e-learning platform which aims to facilitate an easy interaction between faculty members and students within Institute through digital mediums in this pandemic world. This has become the need of the hour to ensure we have a perfect remote conversation and help us communicate properly.

Note

Website is designed for faculty and management people. It is flexible to you and has a really great user experience.
